About kuppam

Kuppam area was ruled byangundi Zameendarrs from 1066 AD to 1950 AD. There is a historical place called Kangundi 11 Km away from Kuppam, which has a fort, wherefrom the Zameendars ruled the area. They were known as Kangundi Zameendars. About 28 rulers established Zameendars system in about 360 villages.
